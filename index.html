We are Pakistan's leading industrial powerhouse specializing in the trading of a diverse range of industrial equipment, encompassing pipeline fittings, high-pressure pumps, lifting equipment, fire safety systems, boiler parts, and industrial instrumentation.

Fueled by a team of highly qualified professionals, we are committed to delivering optimal solutions. From conceptual design to seamless commissioning and the fabrication of MS structures, we offer comprehensive services that cater to your needs.

At HAQ Engineering, we take pride in our expertise across multiple domains. We are a holistic industrial entity, seamlessly integrating design, production, sales, installation, and services, providing a complete line of materials and solutions.
